## Introduction

[[https://github.com/abinit/abinit_web/blob/main/docs/servers.md]]
and
[[https://github.com/abinit/abinit_web/blob/main/docs/builder.md]]
numerous tests contained in ~abinit/tests are executed, and compared with
reference files.
